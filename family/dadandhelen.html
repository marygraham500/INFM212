About Jack and Helen Graham

My father and my stepmother, Helen, live here in Atlanta, and we see them often (pre-Covid). My dad was originally a Presbyterian minister, but 25 years ago he became a psychotherapist. Helen was also a psychotherapist until she retired this past year. They love traveling all over the world to visit their children and grandchildren. They can't wait for the pandemic to be over, so they can resume their travels!
